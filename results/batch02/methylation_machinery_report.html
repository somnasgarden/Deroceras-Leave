5. Key Findings

  • DNMT1 present, no DNMT3 — maintenance-only methylation, no de novo writer.
  • TET2 + TET3 — active demethylation pathway complete.
  • TDG + APEX1 + GADD45 family — base excision repair (BER) demethylation pathway present.
  • HELLS (×2) — chromatin remodeler that facilitates DNA methylation present.
  • UHRF1 (×2) + CFP1 — methylation readers/recruiters present.
  • Absent: DNMT3A/B/L, TET1, MeCP2, MBD1, MBD3 — streamlined toolkit compared to vertebrates.
