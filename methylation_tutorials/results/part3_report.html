Methods

Tutorial adaptation notes:

  • Original tutorial: human Alzheimer's study (GEO: GSE46644), hg38 genome
  • This adaptation: D. laeve tail regeneration, custom genome (1.78 Gb, 31 chromosomes)
  • Input: Bismark CpG_report.txt files (not .cov files) — required strand collapsing
  • Differential methylation: DSS without smoothing (2M CpG subset for local execution)
  • Coverage filter: ≥5 reads per CpG in all samples
  • Significance: FDR < 0.05, |methylation difference| > 10%
  • Annotation: custom GFF (no TxDb available for non-model organism)
  • GO/KEGG enrichment: skipped (no database for D. laeve)
